# Food-Delevery-App-Data-analysis
This project analyzes the Zomato restaurant dataset to uncover insights about customer preferences, restaurant performance, and market trends. The process begins with data cleaning, where missing values are handled, duplicates are removed, and inconsistent formats are corrected to ensure data accuracy and reliability.

After preparation, exploratory data analysis is conducted to examine ratings, cuisines, pricing, locations, and online delivery options. The analysis identifies factors that influence restaurant ratings, popular cuisine types, pricing patterns, and city-wise distribution of restaurants.

Key metrics such as average ratings, cost trends, and service availability are evaluated to understand what drives customer satisfaction and business success.

Visualizations are used to present findings clearly, highlighting patterns and comparisons across locations and categories.

Overall, the project demonstrates how structured data cleaning and analysis can transform raw restaurant data into actionable insights that support better decision-making for both restaurant owners and customers.
